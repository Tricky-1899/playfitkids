What We Do

PlayFit delivers structured sport-based sessions that combine fun and progression. Children develop coordination, confidence and teamwork through purposeful movement.

Development Pillars

Movement Foundations

Fundamental locomotor skills are introduced and reinforced so children build a strong base for all sports and activities.

Coordination & Reaction

Sessions include drills and games that challenge balance, agility and reaction time to sharpen kids’ movement coordination.

Confidence Through Challenge

We create safe opportunities for kids to push boundaries, helping them discover personal growth through supportive competition.

Teamwork & Social Growth

Group activities nurture collaboration, communication and empathy so children learn to succeed together and celebrate each other.

From the Founder

“I started PlayFit Kids because I love seeing kids be active, compete and enjoy sport, but I also know there’s a better way to do it than just letting them run around for an hour.

I’ve spent most of my life in sport. I grew up playing multiple sports, went on to play professional basketball overseas, and have spent years coaching junior basketball teams. Through those experiences, I’ve seen how much confidence comes from learning to move well. Kids don’t just need activity; they need a bit of direction so that the fun actually builds confidence and skill.”
