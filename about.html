About PlayFit Kids

Learn about our founder, our philosophy and why we’re passionate about purposeful play.

Our Story

PlayFit Kids began with a simple observation: children love to move, play and compete, but unstructured activity alone doesn’t always lead to growth. Our founder, having spent a lifetime immersed in sport—from playing multiple sports as a child to a professional basketball career overseas—recognised the confidence that comes from mastering movement. Years of coaching junior basketball revealed that kids thrive when fun and structure work together. That’s why PlayFit Kids focuses on purposeful sessions where every drill and game helps build skills, confidence and joy.

Professional Background

After years competing at a high level in Australia and overseas, our founder brings elite sporting experience into every session. Exposure to diverse coaching styles and training methods has influenced the PlayFit approach to fostering well-rounded, confident young athletes.

Human Movement Degree

A degree in Human Movement underpins our program design. Evidence‑based principles guide how we structure activities, ensuring that progression and physical literacy development are at the heart of every session.

Junior Coaching Experience

Years of coaching junior basketball teams have shaped our understanding of what kids need to develop. Our coaches know how to encourage effort, cultivate teamwork and help children discover the joy of improvement.

Our Philosophy

At PlayFit Kids we believe fun is best when supported by structure. We deliver sessions where children enjoy themselves while steadily developing physical literacy and social growth. Our approach balances challenge and encouragement so every child can participate, progress and feel proud of what they achieve.

PlayFit Kids offers structured sport and movement programs that fuel fun and build skills.
